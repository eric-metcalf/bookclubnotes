  <span class="date-number">Date Three</span>
  <h2>Let's Get It On</h2>
  <p class="theme">Sex &amp; Intimacy</p>

  <p>Most couples have never actually talked about sex — they've only had it. Great lovers
  are <em>made</em> through conversation, not discovered.</p>

  <h3>Exercises</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Separately <strong>write down what great sex means to you</strong> — emotionally, physically, spiritually — then trade and discuss.</li>
    <li>Share <strong>your sexual history with each other</strong>: favorite memories together, moments of closeness, times you felt distant.</li>
    <li>Talk about <strong>how you like to be approached</strong>, what builds desire for you, and what kills it.</li>
    <li>Create a <strong>shared "love map" of intimacy</strong>: fantasies you're curious about, things you want to try, things that are off the table.</li>
    <li>Agree on a <strong>ritual of connection</strong> — a cue, a gesture, a habit that invites intimacy without pressure.</li>
  </ol>
