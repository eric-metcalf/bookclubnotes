  <span class="date-number">Date Eight</span>
  <h2>A Lifetime of Love</h2>
  <p class="theme">Dreams</p>

  <p>The final date is about legacy: the story you want to tell, together, at the end of
  your life. Honoring each other's dreams is the deepest act of love.</p>

  <h3>Exercises</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Each of you writes down <strong>three lifetime dreams</strong> — things you want to do, be, experience, or leave behind.</li>
    <li>Take turns being the <strong>dream-teller</strong> and the <strong>dream-catcher</strong>: one shares, the other listens without fixing.</li>
    <li>Ask: <strong>What does this dream mean to you?</strong> Where does it come from? What would it cost to let it go?</li>
    <li>Identify <strong>one dream you can honor together</strong> in the next year — and one small step toward it this month.</li>
    <li>Write a <strong>shared mission statement</strong>: if our relationship had a purpose, it would be ______.</li>
  </ol>
