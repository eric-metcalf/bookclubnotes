## About the Book

<div class="about-card" markdown="1">

*I Will Teach You to Be Rich* is structured as a six-week action plan. Each chapter
ends with a concrete set of to-dos: optimize your credit cards, open the right
bank accounts, automate your paychecks, pick a target-date fund, and define what
a "rich life" actually means to you.

### The six weeks at a glance

1. **Optimize your credit cards** — use them as a tool, negotiate fees, build your score.
2. **Beat the banks** — open a no-fee checking and high-yield savings account.
3. **Get ready to invest** — open 401(k) and Roth IRA accounts, pick allocations.
4. **Conscious spending** — build a guilt-free plan that funds what you love.
5. **Automate it** — set up the money flow so it runs without you.
6. **The rich life** — define your vision and start spending extravagantly on it.

### How we'll read it

The chapters are hands-on, so we'll pair each meeting with whichever action items
fit. Expect some share-outs — what you automated, what you renegotiated, what
your "rich life" looks like.
